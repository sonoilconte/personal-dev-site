Project - Safeway One Touch Fuel App

Features

-Pay and activate the gas pump from your iOS or Android device
-Locate gas stations near you
-Apply rewards points for a discount
-Get a receipt in the app

Technologies

-NodeJS backend deployed on Azure Functions serverless platform
-CosmosDB NoSQL database
-Push notifications from the backend to device on pump activation and when receipt is ready
-Azure Devops for CI/CD
